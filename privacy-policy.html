Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Island Algorithms ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website islandalgorithms.co.uk, use our services, or interact with custom AI software we have developed. Island Algorithms provides custom AI solutions including chatbots, workflow automation tools, data analytics systems, and other bespoke AI software for businesses.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide to Us

When you contact us or request our services, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Business name and details
  • Information about your project requirements

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Referring website
  • Device information

This information is collected through cookies and similar technologies. See Section 5 for more information about cookies.

1.3 Information Processed Through Our AI Solutions

When you interact with custom AI software we have developed (such as chatbots, automation tools, or data analytics systems), the following data may be collected and processed:

  • Text inputs, queries, and messages you submit to the AI system
  • Contact details collected via lead capture or intake forms embedded in AI tools
  • Usage data and interaction logs generated by the AI system
  • Any files, documents, or data you upload to or process through the AI system

This data may be transmitted to third-party AI service providers (such as OpenAI or Anthropic) for processing. Please see Sections 4 and 4A for further details on how this data is shared and safeguarded.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and provide customer service
  • Deliver our services and fulfil our contractual obligations
  • Develop, test, and maintain custom AI software on your behalf
  • Send you project updates, invoices, and service-related communications
  • Improve our website and services
  • Analyse website usage and performance
  • Comply with legal obligations

We will not use your personal information for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (UK GDPR)

We process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Contractual necessity: To provide services you have requested
  • Legitimate interests: To improve our services and communicate with potential clients
  • Consent: For cookies and marketing communications (where applicable)
  • Legal compliance: To meet tax, accounting, and regulatory requirements

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information with:

  • Service providers: Such as email hosting providers, payment processors, or analytics services, who assist us in operating our website and business. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data.
  • AI service providers: Such as OpenAI, Anthropic, or other AI platform providers whose APIs or services may be used to power the custom AI solutions we build. Data submitted to these providers is processed in accordance with their own privacy policies and data processing agreements.
  • Infrastructure providers: Such as Cloudflare, Vercel, and hosting services that provide the technical infrastructure for our website and AI solutions.
  • Legal authorities: If required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • Business transfers: In the event of a business sale, merger, or acquisition, your personal data may be transferred to the new entity. We will notify you of any such change and ensure the receiving party continues to process your data in accordance with this policy.

4A. International Data Transfers

Some of our third-party service providers, including AI platform providers (such as OpenAI and Anthropic), cloud infrastructure providers (such as Cloudflare and Vercel), and analytics services, are based outside the United Kingdom, primarily in the United States. This means your personal data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside the UK.

Where we transfer personal data outside the UK, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, which may include:

  • Transfers to countries deemed adequate by the UK Secretary of State
  • UK International Data Transfer Agreements (IDTAs) or the UK Addendum to EU Standard Contractual Clauses
  • The provider's binding data processing agreements and appropriate technical safeguards

You may request further details about the safeguards we use by contacting us at david@islandalgorithms.co.uk.

4B. Our Role as Data Processor and Data Controller

Depending on the context, Island Algorithms may act as either a data controller or a data processor:

  • Data Controller: We act as a data controller for personal data collected directly through our website, enquiry forms, and direct communications with us. This means we determine the purposes and means of processing that data.
  • Data Processor: When we build and operate custom AI software on behalf of a client, we typically act as a data processor. In this role, we process personal data only on the instructions of the client (the data controller), and in accordance with a Data Processing Agreement. If you interact with an AI system we have built for a third-party client, that client's privacy policy governs how your data is used.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies to improve your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device.

5.1 Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function properly
  • Analytics cookies: To understand how visitors use our website (e.g., Google Analytics, if enabled)

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Secure HTTPS encryption for data transmission
  • Access controls and password protection
  • Regular security reviews
  • Secure API key management and environment variable protection for AI service integrations
  • Use of reputable, enterprise-grade AI service providers with their own robust security measures

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law:

  • Contact enquiries: Up to 2 years after last contact
  • Project data: For the duration of the project plus 6 years for legal/tax purposes
  • Website analytics: Anonymised data may be retained indefinitely

8. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Right to restrict processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent

To exercise these rights, please contact us at david@islandalgorithms.co.uk. We will respond within one month.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. Please review their privacy policies before providing personal information.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
